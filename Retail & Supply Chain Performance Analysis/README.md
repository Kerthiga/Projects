# Retail & Supply Chain Performance Analysis

## Problem Statement
The objective of this project is to analyze retail inventory and sales data to identify stock issues, improve inventory efficiency, and support better business decisions.

---

##  Dataset
This **Retail Store Inventory and Sales Dataset** includes:
- Product categories  
- Inventory levels  
- Pricing details  
- Units sold (sales data)

  Dataset Link : https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sandhyapeesara/retail-store-inventory

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI (Dashboard visualization)

---

## Key Analysis
- Product-wise sales performance  
- Inventory level monitoring  
- Stockout identification  
- Revenue contribution by category  

---

## Key Metrics (KPIs)
- Total Units Sold  
- Total Revenue  
- Inventory Level  
- Stockout Count  
- Inventory Turnover  

---
## Dashboard 1: Overview Dashboard
Focus:
- High-level business performance  

KPIs:
- Total Revenue  
- Total Units Sold  
- Inventory Level  

Key Insights:
- Provides a quick snapshot of overall performance  
- Helps identify general trends and issues  

---

##  Dashboard 2: Retail Sales Dashboard
Focus:
- Product and sales performance  

Key Metrics:
- Revenue by Months
- Units Sold by Category  

Key Insights:
- Identifies top-performing products  
- Highlights sales distribution across categories  

---

## Dashboard 3: Supply Chain Dashboard
Focus:
- Delivery and stock availability  

Key Metrics:
- Delivery Trend 
- Order Fulfillment by Category  

Key Insights:
- Stockouts impact sales and operations  
- Delivery gaps indicate supply chain inefficiencies  

---

## Dashboard 4: Inventory Dashboard
Focus:
- Inventory management  

Key Metrics:
- Inventory Levels by Product  
- Inventory Turnover  

Key Insights:
- Helps track stock levels  
- Identifies fast-moving vs slow-moving products  

---

## Recommendations
- Maintain safety stock for high-demand items  
- Track inventory levels regularly  
- Optimize reorder strategies
